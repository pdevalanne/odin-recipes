Slop

Worse than mushy bread!

   Ingredients

  • Forgotten fridge leftovers - just one, or a variety
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • (optional) Scrapings from the bottom of your fridge

Instructions

  1. Dump your leftovers into a large microwaveable bowl
  2. Mix thoroughly using a fork or wisk, adding tepid water if needed to achieve sloppiness
  3. Add salt and sugar to taste
  4. Heat in microwave until it boils over, at least 5 minutes
    (remove with caution - silicone oven mitts are recommended)
  5. If you have fridge scrapings to add, sprinkle them on top

Enjoy!
