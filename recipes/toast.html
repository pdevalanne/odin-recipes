Toast

A delicious alternative to mushy bread!

   Ingredients

  • Bread
  • (optional) Topping

   Equipment

  • Electric toaster
  • Power source
  • Plastic or wooden tongs (no metal, or you could electrocute yourself)

Instructions

  1. Remove one slice of bread from bag
    If your bread is not presliced, see Sliced Bread recipe.
  2. Place in toaster. If your toaster is vertical, drop it in the slot. If you have a toaster oven, place it on the middle rack
  3. Adjust your toaster setting to desired darkness. Anything darker than 'golden' is a waste of bread.
  4. Push the lever to start the toasting.
  5. Carefully watch the toasting action. If a burnt smell appears, immediately eject the toast.
  6. Once the toast has "popped", extract it with your tongs.

Set out on a plate or board. Serves 1 - or 1,000 if you can swing a miracle.
